Elden Ring

What is it?

Elden Ring takes place in the realm of the Lands Between, sometime after the destruction of the titular Elden Ring and the scattering of its shards, the Great Runes. Once graced by the Ring and the Erdtree which symbolizes its presence. We will then see how it goes.

Gameplay?

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game played in a third-person perspective and features elements similar to those found in its predecessors, the Souls series as well as Bloodborne and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, with gameplay focusing on combat and exploration.

Release?

Elden Ring is being developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. It was announced at E3 2019 for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. It was in June 2021 trailer announced a release date of January 21, 2022.
